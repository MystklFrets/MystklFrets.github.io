Clay Bar and Wax

Protect your vehicle's paint

Car detail experts recommend clay bar and wax treatment 4-6 times per year. Restore that showroom shine your car once had by giving it the treatment it needs. Many think their car is clean when they take it in for a car wash and wax, but dont be fooled. With your fingertips, feel the surface carefully and compare to a clay bar finish. If you haven't clay bar'd your car in more than a year, chances are the surface of your car is loaded with fallout and other particles. Let us handle the work and enjoy your new shine!

  • Products: Meguiars, Mothers
  • Tip of the day: Apply the clay bar small sections at a time
  • Duration: 1 - 3 Hours (depending on vehicle size)

Hot Water Extraction

Upholstery and carpet cleaning

Remove stubborn stains and odors from your car's interior by shampoo and extraction vaccum. It has been proven that detailing your car interior areas such as mats, seats, vents and carpet of your car improves the circulating air quality.

  • Products: Meguiars, Mothers, Turtle Wax
  • Tip of the day: Always clean the car interior as the final step of your detail. Saves having to do it again if you're constanly going in and out.
  • Duration: 1 - 2 Hours (depending on vehicle size)

Degrease and Dress Process

Because clean is not good enough

You may not have noticed, but your car tires can look good yet still have a ton of grease accumulated around it. We use a degreasing and rinse method that allows the applied dressing to remain on your tires longer. With our treament, your tires will look like new for weeks!

  • Products: Meguiars, Mothers, Armor-All
  • Tip of the day: If you don't have some of the expensive tire degreasers, try dish soap. One of our life hacks!
  • Duration: 20 - 30 Minutes

Headlight Restoration

Cloudy headlights reduce road visibilty

If your car is over 2 years old or if you travel across states often, chances are there's a cloudy build-up forming on the surface of your car's headlights. Our headlight restoration process will make those headlights sparkle once again!

  • Products: Meguiars, Mothers, Turtle Wax
  • Tip of the day: You can also use a clay bar to remove build-up from a headlight.
  • Duration: 30 - 45 Minutes

Trim Restoration

Bringing back to life those surfaces that once gave your car some pizzazz

Your car has many trim areas that are often neglected or overlooked. In between windows and doors, the fender underside, the top lining, the back lining or the front grill area has black trim that usually starts to fade after years of normal wear. Our touch-up process leaves these area standing out with a clean gloss.

  • Products: Meguiars, Mothers, Turtle Wax
  • Tip of the day: Treat the trim as you would the tires. First degrease the area then apply the protectant which will now last longer.
  • Duration: 10 - 15 Minutes

Air Freshners

Some smell good

Let's not forget the most immportant step of any car detail, making sure it smells good. There's nothing like driving your freshly detailed whip down the road to the smell of vanilla or new car smell or pine. We have over 15 selections to choose from.

  • Products: Little Trees and California Scents
  • Tip of the day: When you air freshner starts to lose its smell, simply wet it to gain a few more days of smell good.
  • Duration: 2 Minutes
